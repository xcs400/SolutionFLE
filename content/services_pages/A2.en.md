## Develop your oral fluency

Conversation is at the heart of language learning. Our workshops are designed to help you overcome your fear of speaking.

### Varied topics
Each session covers a different topic: culture, current events, gastronomy, travel, etc.

### Objectives
- Improve your pronunciation (phonetics)
- Enrich your everyday vocabulary
- Gain fluency and confidence
